Eco Quest

Eco Quest is an educational adventure game that teaches players about environmental conservation. Players explore diverse ecosystems, solve puzzles, and make choices that impact the virtual world's ecology.

My Role

  • Educational Content Designer
  • Puzzle Designer
  • UI/UX Designer

Key Features

  • 5 distinct ecosystems to explore and restore
  • Choice-based narrative with multiple endings
  • Mini-games teaching ecological concepts
  • Collaborative multiplayer missions

Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine
  • C#
  • Adobe Illustrator for 2D art
  • Twine for narrative branching
